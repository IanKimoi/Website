What We Do

These are some of the way we leverage the power of GIS to aid in forest restoration

Forest Management

GIS enhances forest management by assisting land owners and forest managers to evaluate and analyze the species diversity, age and size of timber, timber density, and volume. We are able to store this data in a geographic information system which allows us to generate forest management plans, prescribe a timber sale, update inventories based on a harvest, and assess for insect and disease threat.

Global Forest Watch

We work with other experts through an integrated forest watch online platform to monitor forests and make informed decisions. Collaborating with other specialists helps in brainstorming ideas and taking necessary action with regard to the environmental aspects.

Environmental Assessment

Analyzing the effects of a proposed development project on forests and the environment is important to avoid destroying our planet.
